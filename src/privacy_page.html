Your Privacy

You should be able to make the privacy choices that are right for you. We want to make sure you know where your privacy controls are and how to adjust them. For example, our audience selector tool lets you decide who you share with for every post. We develop controls based on feedback from around the world.

Your information is secure !!!

We use one way hashing password to encrypt your password, and the other data is stored outside of the root folders so you can feel relaxed and enjoy our application.

You can manage your information !!!

You own the information you share on InstaKilogram. This means you decide what you share and who you share, and you can change your mind. That's why we give you tools for deleting anything you've posted. We remove it from your timeline and from our servers.

Data Policy

We need to keep tracks and take data from your current IP to uniquely identify you on the world wide web. We will collect your name and account data, your pictures, cookies, information about the device you use as well as location information in order to help us improve our performance.

Cookies Privacy

A Cookies Policy is a policy that provides users with detailed information about the types of cookies a website uses, how these cookies are used, and how users can control cookies placement through limiting or forbidding a website to place cookies on his/her electronic device.

Normally, the Cookies Privacy will be included in the Cookies popup.
